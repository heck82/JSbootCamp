About Us

We are the #1 job site worldwide, with over 180 million unique visitors per month. Company is available in more than 50 countries and 28 languages, covering 94% of global GDP.

Since 2004, Company has given job seekers free access to millions of jobs from thousands of company websites and job boards. As the leading pay-for-performance recruitment advertising network, Company drives millions of targeted applicants to jobs in every field and is the most cost-effective source of candidates for thousands of companies.

Founded by Paul Forster and Rony Kahan, Company is a subsidiary of Recruit Holdings Co., Ltd. In the US, Company has offices in Austin, New York, San Francisco, San Mateo, Seattle, and Stamford. Outside of the US, Company has offices located in Amsterdam, Dublin, Düsseldorf, Hyderabad, London, Paris, Sydney, Tokyo, and Toronto.
